1. Introduction

GhostBridge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our ultra-secure anonymous messaging application.

Our Core Principle: We implement Zero-Knowledge Architecture, meaning we cannot access your messages even if we wanted to. Your privacy is built into the app's foundation.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Message Content: We never see your messages due to end-to-end encryption
  • Personal Identity: No names, emails, or phone numbers required
  • Contact Lists: We don't access your contacts
  • Location Data: No GPS or location tracking
  • Advertising IDs: No tracking for ads or analytics

2.2 Technical Information We May Collect

  • Device Fingerprint: Anonymous device identifier for security purposes
  • Ghost Codes: Temporary anonymous identifiers (deleted after use)
  • Connection Metadata: Encrypted timestamps for message delivery
  • Security Logs: Intrusion detection data (no personal info)

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Generate anonymous Ghost Codes for messaging
  • Route encrypted messages between users
  • Implement "Burn After Reading" message destruction
  • Detect and prevent security threats

3.2 Security Purposes

  • Intrusion detection and prevention
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Emergency security protocols
  • Anti-forensics and data protection

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Automatic Data Destruction

  • Messages: Deleted immediately after reading ("Burn After Reading")

  • Metadata: Wiped with 5-pass secure deletion

5.2 Emergency Burn Protocol

Users can trigger emergency data destruction that instantly wipes all associated data from our servers with military-grade overwriting techniques.

7. No Data Sharing or Sale

We NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share messages with law enforcement (we can't - they're encrypted)
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Create user profiles or analytics
  • Cooperate with mass surveillance programs

8. Children's Privacy

GhostBridge is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your encrypted data may be processed in various countries where our service providers operate. All data remains encrypted and anonymous regardless of location.

10. Your Privacy Rights

10.1 What You Can Do

  • Delete Data: Use emergency burn feature
  • Anonymous Usage: No account required
  • Control Ghost Codes: Generate new codes anytime
  • Message Control: All messages auto-delete after reading

10.2 Limitations

Due to our Zero-Knowledge Architecture, we cannot provide data portability or detailed data access requests - we simply don't have access to your encrypted messages.

11. Security Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident:

  • Our intrusion detection system will activate automatic protection
  • All potentially affected data will be emergency burned
  • Users will be notified through the app if possible
  • No personal data can be compromised due to encryption

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Significant changes will be announced in the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Legal Compliance

13.1 Law Enforcement

While we comply with lawful requests, our Zero-Knowledge Architecture means we cannot decrypt messages or provide user communications even if legally compelled.

13.2 Transparency

We will publish transparency reports if we receive legal requests, though we expect few due to our technical inability to access user data.
